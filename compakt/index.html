How does it work?

compakt uses suffix arrays to compress messages and minhashing to condense similar messages.

In the example GIF above, noticed the 2 similar messages "This is a message." and "This is an message.". compakt notices that the messages are similar enough and thus condensed them together.

Is this "sentence-compression"?

Technically it is because we're reducing the length of a sentence while maintaining its meaning. However, the algorithms do not look at any semantics of the original message. We just happen to retain the meaning because it's being uttered more than once. This also assumes all messages on Twitch chat are well-formed sentences.

Is this "data-compression"?

Yes but at a much more simplistic level. Advanced techniques of compression prioritize data-size (of course) which is extremely beneficial for storage and transit but not for human-consumption.
